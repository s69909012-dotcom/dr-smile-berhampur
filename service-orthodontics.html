Crooked or misaligned teeth are not just a cosmetic concern. They make it harder to clean your teeth properly, increasing the risk of cavities and gum disease. Orthodontic treatment straightens teeth, improves your bite, and gives you a confident smile — at any age.

What is Orthodontics?

Orthodontics is the branch of dentistry that deals with correcting the position of teeth and jaws. Braces and aligners apply gentle pressure over time to gradually move teeth into the correct position. The result is straighter teeth, a better bite, and a more attractive smile.

Signs You May Need Braces

Crowded teeth — teeth overlapping or twisted because there is not enough space in the jaw.

Gaps between teeth — spaces that affect appearance and allow food to get trapped easily.

Overbite or underbite — upper or lower teeth sticking out too far, affecting chewing and appearance.

Crossbite — upper and lower teeth not meeting properly when biting.

Difficulty cleaning teeth — if crooked teeth make it hard to brush and floss properly.

The Braces Process

1

Consultation

The orthodontist examines your teeth, takes X-rays and photographs, and recommends the best treatment option for your case.

2

Fitting

Braces are fitted to your teeth. The process is painless — only mild pressure is felt. Takes about an hour.

3

Regular Adjustments

You visit every few weeks for adjustments. The wires are tightened gradually to keep teeth moving in the right direction.

4

Braces Removal

Once treatment is complete, braces are removed. A retainer is given to maintain the new position of your teeth.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: At what age should my child get braces?

An orthodontic assessment is recommended between ages 7 and 10. Treatment usually starts between 11 and 14. However, adults can also get braces at any age — it is never too late.

Q: Are braces painful?

Braces cause mild soreness for a day or two after fitting and after each adjustment. This is normal and settles quickly. Pain relief medication helps if needed.

Q: How long does orthodontic treatment take?

Treatment duration depends on the complexity of the case. Simple cases may take less than a year. More complex cases may take longer. Your orthodontist will give you a clear timeline at the consultation.

Q: Can adults get braces in Berhampur?

Absolutely. More and more adults are choosing orthodontic treatment. Ceramic braces and clear aligners are especially popular among adults in Berhampur as they are less noticeable.

Q: What foods should I avoid with braces?

Hard foods like sugarcane and nuts, sticky foods like toffee and chewing gum, and very crunchy foods should be avoided as they can damage braces. Your dentist will give you a full list after fitting.
