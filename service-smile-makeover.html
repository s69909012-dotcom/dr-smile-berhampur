Your smile is often the first thing people notice about you. If you feel self-conscious about your teeth — whether they are stained, chipped, uneven or missing — a smile makeover can change that completely. It is not about perfection. It is about giving you a smile you feel confident showing.

What is a Smile Makeover?

A smile makeover is a personalised combination of cosmetic dental treatments designed to improve the appearance of your smile. There is no one-size-fits-all approach — every smile makeover at Dr. Smile Dental Clinic in Berhampur is planned specifically for each patient based on their concerns, goals and budget.

What Concerns Can a Smile Makeover Fix?

🟡

Yellow or stained teeth — caused by tea, coffee, tobacco or aging. Whitening or veneers can restore a bright, natural colour.

💔

Chipped or cracked teeth — dental bonding or veneers can restore the shape and appearance of damaged teeth.

📏

Uneven or short teeth — teeth that are different lengths or shapes can be corrected with contouring or veneers.

↔️

Gaps between teeth — dental bonding, veneers or orthodontics can close gaps and create a more even smile.

🕳️

Missing teeth — dental implants or bridges restore missing teeth so your smile looks complete again.

😬

Crooked teeth — braces or aligners straighten teeth as part of a complete smile transformation.

Treatments Included in a Smile Makeover

✨

Teeth Whitening

Professional whitening to brighten stained or yellow teeth. Quick and effective — often the first step in a smile makeover.

🪄

Dental Veneers

Thin porcelain shells bonded to the front of teeth to correct shape, colour and size. Natural-looking and long-lasting.

🔵

Dental Bonding

Tooth-coloured composite resin applied to repair chips, cracks or gaps. Quick, affordable and effective for minor corrections.

👑

Dental Crowns

Full coverage crowns for badly damaged or discoloured teeth that cannot be fixed with veneers alone.

😁

Orthodontics

Braces or aligners to straighten crooked teeth as part of a comprehensive smile transformation.

🦷

Dental Implants

Permanent replacement for missing teeth — a complete smile cannot have gaps. Implants restore function and appearance.
