Who Is Teeth Whitening Suitable For?

Professional teeth whitening at Dr. Smile Dental Clinic in Berhampur is suitable for most adults with healthy teeth and gums. It is particularly effective for staining caused by tea, coffee, tobacco, and natural ageing.

Whitening is not suitable for: children under 18, pregnant or breastfeeding women, people with active gum disease or untreated cavities, or patients with severely damaged enamel. Whitening also does not change the colour of crowns, veneers, or composite fillings — only natural teeth respond. We confirm suitability at your free consultation before any treatment begins.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is professional teeth whitening safe?

Yes. We examine your teeth and gums before starting to confirm whitening is suitable. The agents used are clinically controlled to protect your enamel and gums. Professional whitening under dental supervision is considered safe.

Q: How many sessions does teeth whitening take?

In-clinic whitening is completed in one session of 60–90 minutes. Take-home kits require daily use over 2–4 weeks. The right option depends on your staining level and preference — we advise after your consultation.

Q: Is teeth whitening painful?

Most patients experience no pain. Mild sensitivity for 1–2 days after is normal and settles on its own. If you have sensitive teeth, we offer gentler options. Please mention sensitivity at your consultation so we can plan accordingly.

Q: Does teeth whitening work on crowns or veneers?

No. Whitening only works on natural tooth enamel — not crowns, veneers, or fillings. If you have visible restorations, we will assess suitability and advise on colour-matching options during your consultation.

Q: How long do teeth whitening results last in Berhampur?

Results typically last 6 months to 2 years with proper care. Avoiding heavy tea, coffee, and tobacco and maintaining 6-monthly professional cleaning at our Berhampur clinic extends results significantly. Touch-up treatments are available.
