What Happens During a Checkup?

Many people in Berhampur avoid dental checkups because they are not sure what happens. It is simpler than you think:

1

Examination

The dentist examines all your teeth, gums, tongue and soft tissues for any signs of problems. Takes only a few minutes.

2

X-Ray if Needed

If the dentist spots something that needs a closer look, a quick digital X-ray is taken to confirm.

3

Cleaning

Professional scaling removes tartar from teeth surfaces and between teeth — where your brush cannot reach.

4

Treatment Plan

If any treatment is needed, the dentist explains what it is, why it is needed, and the cost — before starting anything.

Who Should Come for a Checkup?

Everyone! — But especially:

Children should have their first dental visit as soon as their first tooth appears. Adults should come every six months even if there is no pain. Elderly patients need regular monitoring for gum disease and denture fit. Pregnant women can safely visit a dentist and should — dental health affects pregnancy health.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How often should I get a dental checkup in Berhampur?

Once every six months is the standard recommendation. If you have gum disease or other ongoing issues, your dentist may suggest more frequent visits.

Q: Is dental checkup painful?

No. A routine checkup is completely painless. The cleaning process may cause mild sensitivity for some patients, but it is not painful.

Q: How long does a checkup take?

A routine checkup and cleaning usually takes between 30 to 45 minutes at our Berhampur clinic.

Q: Is it safe for children to visit a dentist?

Absolutely. Children should start visiting a dentist as soon as their first tooth appears. Early visits help prevent cavities and build healthy dental habits from a young age.
