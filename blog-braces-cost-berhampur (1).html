Crooked or misaligned teeth are not just a cosmetic issue. We see patients every week who struggle to clean their teeth properly because of crowding, which leads to cavities and gum problems over time. Some patients come to us with jaw pain that started because of a bite that was never corrected. Braces fix all of this — gradually and permanently.

What Are Braces and How Do They Work?

Braces apply gentle, steady pressure to your teeth over months or years. This pressure moves the teeth through the bone into the correct position. The bone around the tooth reshapes itself naturally to hold the new position — this process works the same way in a teenager as it does in a 40-year-old adult.

1

Metal Braces

The most common type we use in Berhampur. Steel brackets are bonded to each tooth and connected by a wire that we tighten at each visit. They work for all types of cases — mild, moderate, and severe — and are the most affordable option available.

2

Ceramic Braces

Work exactly like metal braces but use tooth-coloured brackets — so they are much less noticeable. Many of our adult patients in Berhampur prefer this option because they want effective treatment without the obvious metal look. The cost is a little higher than metal.

3

Clear Aligners

Removable, nearly invisible plastic trays custom-made for your teeth. You switch to a new set every two weeks. No brackets, no wires — patients love the freedom. Best for mild to moderate cases. Not suitable for complex bite corrections.

4

Lingual Braces

Metal braces fitted on the inner (tongue-side) surface of the teeth — completely hidden from view. They work the same as regular braces but need a more specialised fitting. The most discreet fixed option for patients who want zero visibility.

How Much Do 6-Month Braces Cost in India?

Patients often come to us having seen "6-month braces" advertised online and want to know if it is right for them. Here is what we tell them honestly.

6-month braces — also called short-term orthodontics — focus only on the front visible teeth. They do not correct the full bite or move back teeth. Because the scope is limited, the treatment is shorter and less expensive. In India, 6-month braces typically cost between ₹30,000 and ₹80,000 depending on the clinic and complexity.

⚠️ Are 6-Month Braces Right for You?

  • Suitable only for mild crowding or spacing of the front teeth
  • Do not correct bite problems — overbite, underbite, or crossbite
  • If your case is moderate or complex, 6-month braces will not give a complete result
  • The only way to know if you are a candidate is a proper consultation and X-ray — we do this free of charge at our Berhampur clinic

What Affects the Final Cost?

The severity of misalignment, how long treatment takes (anywhere from 6 months to 3 years), whether retainers are included, and the number of adjustment visits all affect the total. Always ask for a full package cost — not just the cost of fitting the braces on day one.

Is 25 Too Late for Braces?

No — not at all. This is one of the most common worries we hear from patients in Berhampur, and it stops a lot of adults from getting treatment that would genuinely improve their life.

We treat patients in their 20s, 30s, and 40s regularly. Adults are often our most committed patients — they keep every appointment, follow instructions carefully, and get excellent results. The biology works the same way at any age.

✓

Teeth Move at Any Age

The process that lets braces move teeth — called bone remodelling — works the same way at 25 or 45. Adult bones are denser, so movement may be a little slower. But the final result is just as good. Age is not a barrier — gum health is what matters.

✓

Adults Are Often Better Patients

In our experience, adult patients tend to be more consistent with wearing aligners, attending adjustment visits, and following aftercare advice. This directly improves the result. Teenage patients sometimes need more reminding — adults generally just get it done.

✓

Modern Braces Are Discreet

Ceramic braces, lingual braces, and clear aligners mean most adults complete treatment without anyone around them ever noticing. You do not have to look like a teenager getting braces — there are discreet options for every lifestyle.

The one thing we always check before starting any adult braces case is gum health. Gum disease or significant bone loss needs to be treated first. A proper consultation will confirm whether you are a good candidate — and in most cases, the answer is yes.

Is ₹5,000 Enough for Braces?

No — and we say this clearly because we have seen what happens when patients go to the wrong place.

We have had patients come to our Berhampur clinic after a bad experience elsewhere — brackets that came off within weeks, wires causing injuries, treatments that were abandoned halfway because the provider disappeared. In every case, the initial price was suspiciously low.

A complete metal braces treatment — the most affordable type available — costs a minimum of ₹25,000. This includes the brackets, wires, all adjustment visits throughout the treatment, and the retainer at the end. Anything significantly below this is missing something.

⚠️ What Happens with ₹5,000 Braces?

  • Uncertified brackets — low-quality metal that may cause irritation or fail mid-treatment
  • Incomplete treatment — the ₹5,000 may only cover fitting, with large extra charges at every visit
  • No follow-up visits — braces that are never tightened do not move teeth — they just sit there and damage enamel
  • No retainer — without a retainer after braces, teeth shift back within months of finishing
  • Removing failed braces, letting the mouth recover, and restarting treatment correctly ends up costing far more than doing it right the first time

If you see a very low price anywhere in Berhampur, just ask what exactly is included. A trustworthy clinic will always be able to tell you — clearly and without pressure.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How much do 6-month braces cost in India?

6-month braces in India cost between ₹30,000 and ₹80,000 on average. They work only for mild front-teeth alignment and are not a replacement for full orthodontic treatment in moderate or complex cases.

Q: How much do braces cost in India?

Metal braces cost ₹25,000–₹50,000, ceramic braces ₹40,000–₹80,000, and clear aligners ₹1,00,000–₹3,00,000. Final cost depends on your case, treatment duration, and what is included in the package. Always get a full written estimate before starting.

Q: Is 25 too late for braces?

No. There is no age limit for braces. We regularly treat adults in their 20s, 30s, and 40s at our Berhampur clinic. As long as your teeth and gums are healthy, braces work effectively at any age.

Q: Is ₹5,000 enough for braces?

No. A complete metal braces treatment — the most affordable option — costs a minimum of ₹25,000 and includes all visits and the retainer. Any offer significantly below this is missing important parts of the treatment.

Q: How long does braces treatment take?

It depends on your case. Simple cases can be done in 6 months. Complex bite corrections may take up to 3 years. Most patients at our clinic complete treatment in 12 to 18 months. We give every patient an estimated timeline after examining their teeth.

Q: Do braces hurt?

There is usually some soreness for 2–3 days after fitting and after each tightening appointment. This is normal and settles quickly. Most patients tell us they adjusted within the first few weeks and barely notice the braces after that.

Q: Are braces available in Berhampur?

Yes. Dr. Smile Dental Clinic in Berhampur offers metal braces, ceramic braces, and clear aligners. We assess every patient individually and recommend the option that suits their case, budget, and lifestyle.
